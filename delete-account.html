01 Delete from inside the app

This is the fastest way and happens immediately.

  1. Open Nest and sign in.
  2. Tap your Profile.
  3. Scroll to Danger zone at the bottom.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Type DELETE in the confirmation field and tap the button.
  6. Confirm one more time on the system prompt.

You'll be signed out automatically. The account, your data, and any solo families you created are removed at that moment.

02 Delete by email request

If you can't access the app — for example, you lost your password and can't sign in — email us at [[CONTACT EMAIL]] from the address used to register the account.

Include the words "delete my account" in the subject line. We'll process the request within 7 business days and reply to confirm.

03 What gets deleted

  • Your profile, email address, and account credentials
  • Families where you are the only member, including all their content (notices, calendar events, shopping lists, birthdays, contacts, meals, expenses)
  • Your expenses, settlements, and balance history across all families
  • Your membership in any shared families
  • Your event attendance records
  • Your past join requests
  • Any feedback or support messages you sent through the app

04 What stays

In shared families where other members remain, the content you created (birthdays, calendar events, shopping items, notices, meals, contacts) stays so your family doesn't lose its data. Your name is removed from it — those items appear as having no specific author after you leave.

This is intentional. The family is still using the app, and removing everyone's data when one person leaves would harm them more than it helps you.

05 Retention & backups

Active systems. Your data is removed from our active database immediately when you confirm deletion in the app, or within 7 business days for email requests.

Backups. Our database provider (Supabase) keeps automated backups for disaster recovery. Your deleted data is purged from these backups within 30 days of deletion.

Crash logs. Anonymized crash and diagnostic reports processed by our crash-reporting provider (Sentry) follow that provider's 90-day retention schedule and are then removed.

Legal & compliance. We may retain a minimal record that an account was deleted (the date, not the identity) where required for legal, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes.

06 Active subscriptions

If you have an active premium subscription, cancel it through your Apple App Store or Google Play account settings before — or after — deleting your account. Nest doesn't process billing directly, so we can't cancel a subscription for you.

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel future billing on these platforms.
